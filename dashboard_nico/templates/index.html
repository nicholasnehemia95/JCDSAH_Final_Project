Olist Customer Segmentation with K-Means Clustering

Classification of Sao Paolo customers Based on RFM (Recency, Frequency, and Monetary) Matrix and K-Means

RFM Definition

  • Recency

    When was the last time a customer purchased a product from date?

  • Frequency

    How often has the customer used our service from set date until now?

  • Monetary

    What is the total monetary value that the customer has spent over the time period?

Why is Customer Segmentation Important?
  • Optimation of Resources

    RFM segmentation allows marketers to target specific clusters of customers with communications that are much more relevant for their particular behavior â€“ and thus generate much higher rates of response, plus increased loyalty and customer lifetime value.

  • Understanding Customer Positions and Movements

    We can understand who are our most valuable customers, and also understand the movement of clusters to evaluate marketing strategies

  • Increase Customer Retention and Decrease Customer Churn

    By using this method, we can see which customers are potential Customers and High Value Customers, through customer relationship Management we can try to convert potential customers into High Value Customers, and retain high Value Customers
